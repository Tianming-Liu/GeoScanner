GeoScanner

A Portable Mobile Sensing Platform

Project Introduction

GeoScanner is an innovative project combining self-designed hardware sensor modules and a mobile application, dedicated to comprehensively monitoring urban environments. Our goal is to achieve high-precision monitoring of various environmental and street view indicators through mobile sensing technology. We aim to popularize this technology at the individual level, creating a more extensive and precise urban measurement network.

Features

  • Self-designed hardware sensor modules
  • Mobile application
  • Multi-parameter monitoring
  • Monitoring route planning

Solutions

  • High-precision environmental monitoring
  • Wide-coverage mobile sensing
  • Data sharing and application

Application Scenarios

  • Healthy living
  • Urban management
  • Academic research
